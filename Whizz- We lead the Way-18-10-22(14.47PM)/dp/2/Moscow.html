Places :

The cosmopolitan capital of Russia, Moscow is home to architectural marvels, magnificent churches, beautiful galleries and museums. Moscow Kremlin is the major attraction of the city followed by Saint Basil's Cathedral, Red Square, Bolshoi Theatre, Lenin's Mausoleum and Kremlin Wall Necropolis. The famous Russian Salad originated from this city. When here, you must try the traditional Borsch - beet and cabbage red soup, and smoked salmon delicacies. The nightlife in Moscow is both laidback and lively depending upon your choice of place. You can enjoy Jazz in Live music bars here or can opt to party hard in the thriving night clubs.
